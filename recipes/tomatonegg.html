Tomato n Eggs

Tomato and eggs is a popular dish in China said to be so simple that it tests the skills of even the most accomplished cook.

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • Eggs

Amount depends on personal taste and size/shape of ingredients.


Steps

  1. Cut the tomatoes into small pieces. No need to skin.
  2. Mix the eggs in a small container.
  3. Fry the eggs first, putting some salt in. Remove the eggs and wash the pan.
  4. Cook the tomatoes until it becomes pasty. Add salt. Add the eggs.
