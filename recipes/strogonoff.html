Strogonoff

No longer made with cats (unsure if once they were), the strogonoff is a russian dish that is popular in Brazil.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast or thigh
  • Spoonful of mayonaise
  • Baby bella mushrooms
  • Whipping cream
  • Spoonful of ketchup
  • Spoonful of mustard

Amount depends on personal taste and size/shape of ingredients.


Steps

  1. Cut the chicken into small, bite size portions.
  2. In a bowl, mix the chicken with the mayonaise.
  3. Cut the baby bellas and put to the side.
  4. Mix the ketchup and mustard and put to the side.
  5. Cook the chicken until seared.
  6. Add the mushroom to absorb the liquid.
  7. Put in the mixed ketchup and mustard.
  8. Mix in the whipping cream.
  9. Serve.
